✨ Offline-First Design: The entire Holy Quran text, translations, Tafsir, and duas are stored directly inside the app. You don't need an internet connection to read, search, or recite.

Why offline matters? Whether you're traveling, in areas with poor connectivity, or simply want to save mobile data — Al-Noor keeps working flawlessly. Your reading progress, bookmarks, and Tasbih counts remain saved on your device so you never lose your place.

  • Instant access to any Surah — no loading delays

  • Prayer times cached for offline reference
